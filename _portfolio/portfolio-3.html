--- title: "Photonic Interfaces for Hybrid Quantum Systems" excerpt: "Short description of portfolio item number 2
" collection: portfolio --- We make efforts to develop efficient photonic interfaces for hybrid quantum systems. We are particularly interested in cavity-enhanced quantum light sources that can generate ultra-bright, long-coherence-length, single-longitudinal-mode photons with well-controlled spectral and temporal properties. Based on that, we also investigate strong interactions between single photons and single material qubits or quantum emitters, including single atoms and quantum dots, mediated by carefully designed optical cavities and photonic structures. These light–matter interfaces can enable deterministic quantum gates, quantum memories, photon–matter entanglement, and scalable hybrid architectures for distributed quantum information processing.
